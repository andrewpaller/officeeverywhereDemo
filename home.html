Where We Are So Far

We have been quite busy visting each destination in the OfficeEverywhere rollout. Check out the stats below to see where we are and where we are headed.

Skype for Business

Skype simply offers a more features with a faster meeting startup with less of a headache. The adoption rate is steadily increasing every month. There have been more than 34M peer to peer messages sent and more than 706K meeting participants in June alone.

Office 2016

Approximately 40K out of 71K machines have installed Office 2016.

Exchange

We hope your migration went smoothly and that you are enjoing your 50GB mailbox. We have migrated just over 66K mailboxes out of 80K!

OneDrive

60TB have been provisioned and so far we have consumed 28TB of storage with 20.3 million documents! Continue to use OneDrive and promote how easy it is to use and collaborate with your teammates. This will pay dividends if your hard drive suddenly crashes. You will be up and running in no time.

SharePoint Team Sites

Starting in August SharePoint Team Sites will be rolling out in a phased approach. LOB team leads have been selected and will be leading this project. Migrating to SharePoint Team Sites will give your team additional features, such as: document storage, co-authoring, mobile access, improved search, and online forms.

Mobile

On June 28th mobile was rolled out to the entire enterprise! 11,500 apps have been downloaded to iOS corporate devices so far. This piece to the OfficeEverywhere puzzle allows you to work the way you want. Visit the mobile page for more information.
